Marriage and age in Jamaica

A STATIN dataset shows what's likely a universal reality

September 21, 2021. Jason DeCambre

Some data scraping and cleaning turned into an interesting find.

Describing human behaviour from the perspectives of biology and behavioural economics has always been controversal.

But the data from Jamaica seem to support the claim that women tend to be most attractive partners as very young adults, while men take longer to accrue resources and therefore attractiveness.

I had started on some further exploratory analysis with other datasets to see how this relationship held as conditions changed, as well as how it interacts with other variables.
